# Who I Am

I'm **Tekrox.eth**, based in Slovenia. I've been active in Web3 for the past 3–4 years, after spending nearly two decades building in Web2. My background isn't purely technical — I come from marketing, operations, and project management — but I've always worked closely with technology, teams, and emerging internet trends. At my core, I'm a builder driven by curiosity about what comes next.

# Background

Before Web3, I founded and ran an in-person coding school that helped people transition into software development careers. The school operated across **12 European countries**, with more than **10,000 students** completing our programs. Our mission was simple: close the gap between industry demand and available developer talent. In 2021, we successfully exited the company.

# Web3 Journey

After the exit, I moved fully into Web3. One of the main projects I worked on was **Punk Domains** — a protocol enabling Web3 communities to launch their own domain extensions, similar in spirit to ENS but designed for community ownership and expansion.

Since then I've explored Web3 social protocols (including Farcaster), DAO participation and governance, community-driven infrastructure, and early experimentation with emerging crypto primitives. Experimentation is a core part of how I work.
